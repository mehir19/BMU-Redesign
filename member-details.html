Biography

Professor Dr. Warren Tate is a renowned Cardiologist with over 25 years of experience in clinical practice and academic research. He has been serving Bangladesh Medical University since its inception, focusing on Interventional Cardiology and Preventive Medicine.

His work primarily revolves around improving cardiac care protocols in South Asia. He has mentored hundreds of postgraduate students and led several national-level health research projects funded by the Ministry of Health.
