Postgraduate Program

MD in Cardiology

A 5-year residency program designed to produce world-class cardiologists through rigorous clinical training and advanced research methodology.

5 Years
Full Time
Intake: July/January

Course Overview

The Doctor of Medicine (MD) in Cardiology at BMU is structured to provide extensive hands-on experience in both non-invasive and interventional cardiology. Students undergo rotations in the CCU, Cath Lab, and Outpatient Departments, mentored by leading specialists in the field.

Research Driven

Mandatory thesis work with access to state-of-the-art genomics labs.

Clinical Excellence

Direct involvement in over 500+ procedures during residency.

Curriculum Structure

Basic Medical Sciences (Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry) Core
General Medicine Rotation Clinical
Introduction to ECG & Non-invasive Diagnostics Technical
Interventional Cardiology & Cath Lab Procedures Advanced
Electrophysiology and Pacemaker Management Advanced
Thesis Submission & Final Viva Final

Admission Requirements

  • MBBS degree from a BMDC recognized institution.
  • Completion of one-year internship.
  • Minimum 2 years of professional experience after graduation.
  • Valid registration with BMDC.
  • Passing score in the BMU Postgraduate Entrance Examination.

Course Fee

৳ 2,50,000 / Total

Includes registration, semester fees, and clinical lab access. Examination fees are separate.
