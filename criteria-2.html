Eligibility & Entry Requirements

General Requirements

Standard Minimum Academic Requirement: Candidates must have obtained average graduation scores of GPA ≥ 3.5 or equivalent grades across all semesters of their foundational medical degrees.

  • Standard Basic Medical Degree

    An MBBS or BDS degree from an institution registered and approved by the Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council (BM&DC).

  • Mandatory Clinical Training

    Completion of exactly 1 year of continuous, supervised clinical internship training on or before the application submission deadline.

  • BM&DC Council Registration

    Must hold an active, valid permanent registration status certificate from the BM&DC.

Degree Specific Criteria

  • Graduation Experience Constraint

    A minimum of 2 years of active professional clinical experience (including standard internship tenure) completed post-graduation.

  • Academic Entry Exam

    Satisfactory performance score in the BMU Written Postgraduate Examination, with a mandatory minimum cut-off mark of 60%.

  • Required Practical Surgery Exposure

    Requires at least 2 years of work experience, with a recommendation for documented residency rotation within surgical units or casualty departments.

  • Physical Dexterity Review

    Surgical streams assess structural physical dexterity competencies as part of the mandatory downstream clinical admission interviews.

  • Basic Science Stream Selection

    Open to both MBBS and BDS graduates focusing on pre-clinical or basic science studies (e.g., Microbiology, Biochemistry, Anatomy).

  • Research Proposal Recommendation

    Candidates are encouraged to present a preliminary 1-page research scope draft detailing their scientific interests during interviews.

  • Shorter Professional Qualification

    Minimum 1 year of clinical experience post-internship. Ideal for working professionals seeking accelerated clinical specialization.
