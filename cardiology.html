About Cardiology

The Department of Cardiology at Bangladesh Medical University (BMU) is a premier center for cardiovascular excellence, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate patient care.

Established in 2015, we have consistently pushed the boundaries of heart care through innovative research and world-class residency programs. Our department is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and interventional facilities to handle even the most complex cardiac conditions.

The Department of Cardiology at Bangladesh Medical University (BMU) is a premier center for cardiovascular excellence, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate patient care.

Professor Dr. Warren Tate

MBBS, FCPS, MD (Cardiology)

Chairman, Cardiology

"Our mission is to bridge the gap between advanced cardiac technology and healthcare accessibility. We don't just treat hearts; we heal lives through continuous learning and dedication."

With over 25 years of experience, Dr. Tate leads our department with a vision for excellence in interventional cardiology and medical education.
